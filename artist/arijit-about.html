Artist Biography

Arijit Singh is an acclaimed Indian playback singer known for his soulful voice and versatility. Gaining fame with the hit song Tum Hi Ho from Aashiqui 2 (2013), he has since become one of Bollywood's most prominent vocalists, known for his emotional depth and classical influences.

Achievements and Awards

Arijit Singh, a celebrated Indian playback singer, has won multiple Filmfare Awards for his contributions to Bollywood music. He is renowned for his emotionally resonant songs, including hits like Channa Mereya, Raabta, and Tum Kya Mile.
