Artist Biography

Shreya Ghoshal is an Indian playback singer known for her versatile voice and emotive performances in Bollywood and regional cinema. She began her career with a debut song in Devdas and has since become one of the most acclaimed voices in Indian music. Shreya's repertoire spans various genres, from classical to contemporary.

Achievements and Awards

Shreya has won 4 National Film Awards and numerous Filmfare Awards for Best Female Playback Singer. She is renowned for her contributions to Indian cinema and music, with a career spanning over two decades.
