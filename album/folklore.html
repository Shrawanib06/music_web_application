Folklore

Release date:

24 July 2020

Genre:

Alternative rock, Indie folk, Chamber pop, Indietronica

Description:

Folklore is the eighth studio album by the American singer-songwriter Taylor Swift. It was a surprise album, released on July 24, 2020, via Republic Records. Following the outbreak of the COVID-19 pandemic in early 2020, Swift canceled the concert tour for her seventh studio album Lover.
