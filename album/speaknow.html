Speak Now

Release date:

25 October 2010

Genre:

Pop rock, Power pop, Country pop

Description:

Speak Now is the third studio album by the American singer-songwriter Taylor Swift, released on October 25, 2010, by Big Machine Records. Swift wrote the album entirely herself within two years while touring to promote her second studio album, Fearless, to reflect on her transition from adolescence to adulthood.
